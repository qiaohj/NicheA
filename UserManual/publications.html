Citing NicheA

To cite NicheA in publications, use
Qiao, H., et al. (2015). NicheA: Creating Virtual Species and Ecological Niches in Multivariate Environmental Scenarios. Ecography: 10.1111/ecog.01961.

In this page, we collected the publications which used NicheA as a part of the analysis process. If your publications are not be included, please send a message to Huijie Qiao. Thank you very much!
